What is Debate Lab?

Debate Lab uses AI to generate a multi-perspective debate on any topic you choose. By reading, questioning, and reflecting on the debate, you will develop stronger argumentative writing skills. The platform is powered by DeepSeek V3.2 — a large language model that generates the debaters, their arguments, and personalised feedback on your writing.

How it works
1
Choose a topic and write an initial argumentative essay.
2
Watch an AI debate between three stakeholders with different perspectives.
3
Take notes and ask questions — challenge the debaters directly.
4
Reflect and revise your essay using insights from the debate.
5
Receive detailed feedback on your revised writing.
